Anna Therapy

Physical Therapy for Infants and Children

15+ years of experience in the US and Europe, specializing in infants with cerebral palsy and developmental delay between 0-3 years of age in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Who is Anna?

Anna is a seasoned pediatric physical therapist, who throughout her carrier has helped and worked with hundreds of little patients. She is passionate about engaging children to help them develop their full potential.

Experience

Anna graduated in Budapest, Hungary as Physical Therapist in 1998. She is a certified Deveny Special Gymnastics Method therapist, as well as an NDT pediatric therapist. Anna moved to California in 2001, and has been working as a California licensed physical therapist in hospitals and outpatient settings since 2003. Anna has worked at the Santa Clara Valley Medical Center, at the California Children Services in San Mateo, The Avalon Academy in Burlingame, and Therapista, a home-based pysical therapy service for kids.

Specialties

Anna uses NDT, DSGM manual technique.
